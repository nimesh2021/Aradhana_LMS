About Us

We are Aradana learners. Our company is one of the most trusted learning institutions in the Rideegama area. We have been providing the most reliable service since 2005. The staff, including the Aradana learners Administrator , provide the reliable service and safe driver training to the client.

VISION

To build on our reputation as the leader of driver training schools in Asia and to remain at the forefront of road safety awareness.

OUR VALUES

  • To promote a safe and courteous driving environment in our community.
  • To observe unsafe driving practices and correct them.
  • To develop effective learning processes for every task we undertake.
  • To accomplish Quality Driver Tutoring through up to date audio and visual techniques.
  • To respect the dignity and protect the integrity of every student.

OUR MISSION

To improve and enhance safety on the road through providing driver training a service that complies with the highest international standards and best practices.
